EMBER & VINE

A symphony of taste and time.

WE ARE ESSENCE

At Ember & Vine, every dish tells a story — one born from fire, earth, and passion. We celebrate the timeless craft of cooking over flame, where ingredients meet smoke and heat to reveal their most authentic flavors.

Our philosophy is simple: honor the ingredient, respect the process, and ignite the senses. We draw inspiration from nature and tradition, blending modern technique with the soulful essence of open-fire cuisine.

Each plate is a tribute to balance — between warmth and refinement, rustic charm and contemporary elegance. Whether it’s a shared meal or a quiet moment with a glass of wine, Ember & Vine invites you to slow down, savor deeply, and rediscover the beauty of simplicity done to perfection.

OUR HISTORY

Born in 1912 from the meeting of fire and earth, Ember & Vine was envisioned as a tribute to the timeless craft of cooking and the beauty of the vineyard.

Our story began with a simple idea — that true flavor is born from contrast: warmth and patience, passion and balance, ember and vine.

Each dish we create reflects this harmony. From the glow of the open flame to the subtle richness of aged wine, every element tells a story of transformation and connection. We honor the land, the seasons, and the artisans who inspire us, bringing their spirit to every table we serve.

At Ember & Vine, dining is not just a meal — it’s a celebration of origin, fire, and soul.
